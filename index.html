Kanyanat (Natalie) Tongprasearth

UX Designer & Researcher

My design process begins with the people. Understanding their needs and values help me contextualize my design as a solution to their goals. A successful design, one that provides the best experience for its users, is what I strive for as a UX Designer and Researcher.

RESEARCH & REFRAME

Through user research, I am able to observe, interact, and immerse to gain insights about the people I'm designing for and the needs I'm solving for. This frames and contextualizes my design as a whole.

IDEATE & PROTOTYPE

Prototypes allow for exploring ideas, testing solutions, gaining feedback, and inspiring other ideas. By working with physical concepts, I can start to assess benefits and drawbacks of my prototype.

TEST & ITERATE

By having the context of the physical, environmental, and social environment, I can learn more about the users' expectations and interactions, which inform future design iterations.
